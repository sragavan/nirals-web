An innovation from an unusal place

Nirals is an education startup based out of a small rural town, Kovilpatti, Tamil Nadu. Founders (Srini & Mythili) wanted to start Nirals from Kovilpatti in 2014 for multiple reasons. One being able to spend less on initial cost and others being able to use lot of talent that is available in rural town. In the last 5 years Nirals has attracted more than 300 customers and have nearly 0.5 million users in the platform. EduNiv is  the flagship product and Other innovations include TagTik and Tolka, all three focus on the education platform. Thabaals is a b2b platform for micro ERP for SMEs.
